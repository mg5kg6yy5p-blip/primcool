Why PrimeCool

Engineered for Reliability.

01 — PM CONTRACT MODEL

Recurring. Not Reactive.

PrimeCool operates on a PM-first contract model. 65–75% of revenue is structured maintenance — scheduled, documented, and delivered on interval. We don't wait for equipment to fail. We build the maintenance program that prevents failure from happening. Corrective work, when it arises, follows the same standard: logged, reported, closed out in writing.

02 — DOCUMENTATION STANDARD

Documented by Default.

Every PM visit generates a digital visit report. Every reading is logged. Every finding is reported within 24 hours. Clients receive complete service records — not a callback saying the work is done. The paper trail is the product. KPI tracking, trend data, and full service history are delivered with every contract, every cycle.

03 — TECHNICAL CAPABILITY

Industrial-Grade. Applied Everywhere.

PrimeCool is founded by a Licensed Stationary Engineer with 10+ years of GMP-regulated utility operations — chiller plants, cooling towers, high-pressure boilers, and pharmaceutical-scale systems. That technical depth doesn't stay in the industrial division. Every technician, every tier, and every client receives the benefit of that standard.
